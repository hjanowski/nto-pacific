About NTO Pacific

Get to know the team behind your premium diving equipment

Our Story

Founded in 2010, NTO Pacific was born out of a passion for diving and a commitment to quality equipment. What started as a small store in coastal California has grown into a trusted brand for professional and recreational divers worldwide.

Our mission is simple: provide premium, reliable diving equipment that enhances your underwater experience while prioritizing safety and performance. We believe that high-quality gear shouldn't be a luxury but a standard for every diver.

Every product in our catalog has been tested in real diving conditions by our team of professionals, ensuring that it meets our rigorous standards before it reaches you. We stand behind our products with pride and confidence.

Meet The Team

Hadas Yaniv

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of diving experience across the world's most challenging dive sites, Hadas founded NTO Pacific to share her passion for quality diving equipment with the community.

Noa Ziglin

Head of Product Development

Noa brings his engineering expertise and diving knowledge together to develop innovative products that merge functionality, comfort, and durability for divers of all levels.

Alon Haze

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on materials or craftsmanship. Each product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards.

Safety

We believe diving should be enjoyable and safe. Our equipment is designed with your safety as the top priority.

Innovation

We continuously explore new technologies and materials to improve our products and enhance your diving experience.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices throughout our supply chain and operations.
